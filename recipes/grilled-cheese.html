Grilled Cheese

Description

A comforting, easy classic that will be ready in under 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • 4 slices of sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 slices of alternative cheese like Swiss or provolone
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Set a pan on medium heat.
  2. Liberally spread mayonnaise on one side of each slice of bread, ensuring that it completely covers the surface (no gaps on the edges!).
  3. Quickly place one slice of bread, mayonnaise side down, on the pan, and top with the cheese slices. Cover with the other slice of bread, mayonnaise side up.
  4. Drop a little bit of water into the pan and cover it with a lid, so that the cheese melts quicker.
  5. Once the cheese looks melty, remove the lid and flip the sandwich.
  6. Continue letting the sandwich cook on each side until the desired brownness is achieved on the bread.
  7. Remove from pan and enjoy!
